Digital Art

Digital art is something I've always been interested in. I started off drawing on a website called ratemydrawings.com and at that time I never thought it would be possible because I only had a mouse to draw with. But there I learnt a lot of skills where I can now apply to photoshop or other programs where I can draw. Traditional Art

I have been drawing traditionally for as long as I can remember. I started when I was able to hold a pencil. From there I have experimented with many different styles of art. I originally started with pen and paper and slowly progressed to acrylics and finally to watercolour.
